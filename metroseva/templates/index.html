An easy-to-use website that guides you around the Delhi metro.

See ancient monuments and fascinating museums without ever worrying how you’re going to get there. Plan routes and favourite journeys for quick access again and again. Delhi Metro does the hard work so you just need to worry about where you want to go next.

Find Stations Fast

See a station on the map you want to route from? Tap it! Want to know which station is closest to you? Use the nearest station feature. And of course, you can search for any station by name even when you’re offline.

 

– Travel Tip –

If you’re going to be making several trips on Delhi’s metro, get a Smart Card from a customer service kiosk which is more convenient and gives you a 10% discount on every journey.

Journey Planner

Give Delhi Metro a start and end point and get routes instantly, even when you’re offline. The app will provide the quickest route and if there is an alternative with fewer changes, Delhi Metro will show that too. The route summary provides information on how long the journey will be, how many lines you’ll need to take and how many stations you pass through to get there.

Step-by-step Guides

See every step of your journey on the Delhi Metro map. Each station is highlighted so you know exactly where to change and when to get on and off. This useful feature is available even when the app is offline.

– Did You Know? –

Every train on the Delhi Metro has a women-only car. This is always situated at the front of the train.

– Travel Tip –

There are luggage restrictions on the Delhi Metro. The DMRC request passengers limit their baggage to 15 Kgs and no taller than 60cm.

– Did You Know? –

Security checks are a normal part of travelling on the Delhi Metro. Bags are x-rayed and passengers may be patted down.
